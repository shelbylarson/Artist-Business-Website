Matthew Watkins is an up-and-coming artist specializing in painting. Matthew is a senior at Trinity Christian Academy in Jackson, TN where is he taking Art III. He attended Governor's School for the Arts for visual art on a full scholarship and was in the class of 2014.

Matthew is proficient in drawings and sketches with graphite and colored pencils, charcoal, paintings with acrylic paint and is beginning to work with oil paint. He enjoys oil paint and painting with his palette knives the most. Matthew has only taken art seriously for 3 years, yet he is very talented and skilled for his inexperience.

You can take a look at his work on the "Gallery" page, and even purchase a piece by contacting Matthew via the "Request an Artwork" page
